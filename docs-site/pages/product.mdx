## What is SolverNet DEX?

**SolverNet DEX** is a next-generation decentralized exchange (DEX) built on the Cardano blockchain. Unlike traditional DEXs where you interact directly with liquidity pools, SolverNet uses an **intent-based model** — you simply tell the system what you want, and it finds the best way to execute your trade.

## Why Choose SolverNet DEX?

### Better Prices Through Netting

When two users want to swap in opposite directions (e.g., User A swaps ADA→TOKEN and User B swaps TOKEN→ADA), SolverNet can **match them directly** without going through a liquidity pool. This means:

- **Zero price impact** on netted trades
- **Lower fees** compared to traditional AMM swaps
- **Better rates** for everyone

### Your Funds, Your Control

SolverNet DEX is **fully non-custodial**:

- Your tokens are held in smart contract escrows — not in anyone's wallet
- You can **cancel** your intent and reclaim your funds at any time
- Every trade is verified by on-chain validators — no one can steal your assets

### Built for Cardano

SolverNet DEX is designed from the ground up for Cardano's unique eUTxO model:

- Smart contracts written in **Aiken** (Plutus V3) — fast and efficient
- No concurrency bottlenecks — multiple trades can be processed simultaneously
- Full support for **native Cardano tokens** (no wrapping needed)
